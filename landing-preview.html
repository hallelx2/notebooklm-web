Features
Built for serious research.
neurology

Contextual RAG pipeline

Semantic chunking, contextual embeddings, query expansion, LLM reranking, and HNSW indexing — your questions find the right answers, not just similar words.

Query: "What biomarkers predict treatment response?"
→ Expanded: "biomarkers treatment response", "predictive molecular markers therapy", "omics therapeutic indicators"
→ Reranked: 12 chunks scored by relevance → top 8 selected
→ Answer: Cited response with [1][2][3] references
